🌊 Must-Visit Places in Kanpur

  • Ganga Barrage: A serene spot to witness the holy Ganges — unmissable, especially for first-time visitors from the South.
  • JK Temple: Peaceful and architecturally stunning.

🛍️ Thoughtful Gifts for Your Child

  • Landmark Bookstore: Pick an inspiring book or study material they’ll appreciate.
  • PANNA: Get them trendy clothes or warm wear for Kanpur winters.
  • Thaggu ke Laddu: Treat them to Kanpur’s iconic sweets before you leave!
